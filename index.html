Scholarly Work

Research Projects

LL.M. Thesis — 2025

Beyond the 1951 Refugee Convention: The Role of Regional Human Rights Courts in the Development of International Refugee Law

Examines the contribution of regional human rights courts to International Refugee Law beyond the 1951 Convention. Analyzes jurisprudence of the European Court of Human Rights, the Inter-American Court, and African Court, evaluating how regional mechanisms strengthen refugee protection through non-refoulement and procedural safeguards.

LL.B. Thesis — 2024

Safeguarding Justice: Call for a Robust Witness Protection Regime in Bangladesh

A doctrinal and socio-legal examination of the gaps in witness protection frameworks in Bangladesh. Proposes legislative and institutional reforms to strengthen the justice system and ensure effective prosecution of serious crimes.

Field Research — Ongoing

Public Participation in Environmental Governance: Enhancing EIA for Sustainable Development in Bangladesh

Investigates the efficacy of Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) processes in Bangladesh, focusing on enhancing public participation mechanisms for more inclusive and sustainable governance outcomes.

Field Research — 2nd & 3rd Year

Issues and Challenges of Child Labor & The Children Act, 2013: Law and Practice

A survey-based study on child labor in hotels, restaurants, and retail stores in Trishal, Mymensingh, alongside a district-level study on the implementation of the Children Act, 2013 in Mymensingh.
